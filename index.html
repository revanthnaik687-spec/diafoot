🏥Medical Treatments
Total contact casting (TCC) — gold standard offloading
Custom orthotic insoles to redistribute pressure
Debridement of callus to reduce stress concentration
Wound dressings (hydrocolloid/foam) if ulcer present
Vascular assessment if healing is delayed
Blood glucose control — critical for tissue repair
👟Footwear & Offloading
Diabetic therapeutic shoes with extra depth
Rocker-bottom soles to reduce forefoot pressure
Avoid walking barefoot — even indoors
Inspect feet daily with mirror or caregiver help
Change footwear every 4–6 hours if prolonged standing
Use padded socks — no tight elastic bands
🌿 Home Remedies & Ayurvedic Tips
🪷
Turmeric paste (haldi) — apply on callus/early sores; anti-inflammatory and antibacterial.
🌿
Neem oil massage — antimicrobial, improves local circulation and prevents infection.
🫙
Warm sesame oil (til oil) foot massage daily — improves peripheral blood flow and nerve health.
🍵
Triphala foot soak — reduces swelling and bacterial load (3× per week, 10 minutes).
🌱
Aloe vera gel — cooling, wound-supportive; do NOT apply on open ulcers.
🚶
Avoid standing on hard floors for more than 20 minutes continuously.
🦵
Legs elevated 15–20 min after long walks to reduce venous pressure.
🍃
Ashwagandha + guduchi decoction — supports nerve health (consult doctor before use).
⚠️ Ayurvedic remedies are complementary care only. Never replace prescribed medication. Always consult your doctor.
